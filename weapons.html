Weapon Guide

Trick weapons are Bloodborne's defining mechanic. Every main weapon has two forms — a compact form and an extended form — switched with a button press mid-combat. This isn't just cosmetic: both forms have completely different movesets, weights, and uses. Watching a skilled player switch fluidly between them is one of the game's great pleasures as a spectator.
